    About This Website

    Diabetes@Home is a startup where we plan to provide digital health solutions to targeted health requirements. Our flagship product is a web app that helps people manage their diabetes in the convenience of their own home, by recording data that can be monitored remotely by their clinician.


    To continue using the features of our app, users will need to be logged in. Please create an account and contact your clinician for further information.
